NUT-FREE BASIL PESTO

NUT-FREE BASIL PESTO

 

PREP TIME: 5 mins

 

COOK TIME: 15 mins

 

TOTAL TIME: 20 mins

This gorgeous pesto is full of color and flavor! Serve on your favorite pasta or crackers for a delicious, healthy meal.

Ingredients
  • 8 oz pasta
  • 1 cup packed basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • Pinch of salt
Directions
  1. Cook the pasta according to instructions on packaging.
  2. In a food processor, pulse garlic and basil together.
  3. Add parmesan cheese, lemon juice, and salt. Pulse again.
  4. Add olive oil and blend. If the mixture is too thick, add more oil or hot water to thin.
  5. When the pasta is ready, mix it altogether and enjoy!
